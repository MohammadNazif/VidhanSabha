Child Safety Standards for Matdaan CRM

Last Updated: June 2026

Macreel Infosoft Pvt. Ltd. is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users of the Matdaan CRM application.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Matdaan CRM has a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, trafficking, sexual exploitation of minors, or any content or behavior that endangers children.

Users are strictly prohibited from:

  • Creating, uploading, sharing, transmitting, or storing Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Engaging in grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors.
  • Using the platform for any activity that harms or threatens children.
  • Sharing content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child abuse or exploitation.

Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior through the application's support or reporting channels.

Macreel Infosoft Pvt. Ltd. reviews reports and may:

  • Remove violating content.
  • Suspend or terminate offending accounts.
  • Report illegal activity to relevant law enforcement authorities when required by law.

Compliance

Matdaan CRM complies with applicable child safety laws and regulations and cooperates with authorized law enforcement agencies regarding reports of child exploitation.
